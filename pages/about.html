This rare disease database aims to provide high quality information on rare diseases. It is maintaining a list of rare diseases and related information to help people find reliable information. Users can search rare disease information in terms of disease name, gene or by disease category. The facial photographs for rare diseases are also available here. The photographs are carefully collected from published articles, hospital foundations, RD blogs and awareness campaigns. This platform can be used for different research purposes including the investigation of relationship between genotype and phenotypic data, analysis of the properties of disease genes, drug repurposing etc.
